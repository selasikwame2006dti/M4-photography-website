What is food photography>>>




Food photography is a genre of photography focused on capturing visually appealing images of food. It is commonly used in advertising, cookbooks, menus, social media, blogs and magazines to showcase food in a way that makes it look appetizing and aesthetically pleasing.

HERE ARE SOME PROMINENT PEOPLE IN THIS FIELD>




1. Dennis Prescott

Dennis Prescott is one of the most famous food photographers in the industry. But he is also a chef and cookbook author. He was once a struggling musician, but then he found his passion for food. His rise to success and fame was quicker than most. That’s because he is one of the hosts of the Netflix show Restaurants on the Edge. The hosts help struggling restaurants start over and find their path to success.
